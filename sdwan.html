MapleCloud SD-WAN Solution

MapleCloud SD-WAN solution is a cutting-edge networking technology designed to optimize and streamline your organization's connectivity. By leveraging software-defined networking principles, MapleCloud SD-WAN offers unparalleled flexibility, security, and performance, empowering businesses to meet the demands of today's dynamic digital landscape.

Intelligent Routing: MapleCloud SD-WAN intelligently routes traffic across multiple network paths, dynamically selecting the most efficient route based on application requirements and network conditions. This ensures optimal performance and reliability for critical applications. Centralized Management: With a centralized management interface, MapleCloud SD-WAN simplifies network administration and monitoring. Administrators can easily configure policies, prioritize traffic, and monitor performance from a single dashboard, streamlining operations and reducing complexity. Secure Connectivity: Security is paramount in today's digital environment. MapleCloud SD-WAN employs advanced encryption protocols and security measures to safeguard data in transit, protecting against threats such as malware, ransomware, and unauthorized access. Additionally, built-in firewall capabilities provide an added layer of defense at the network perimeter.

Benefits of MCT SD-WAN

Ready for multi-cloud

MCT SD-WAN provides OnRamp to multiple public and private cloud which can be interconnected to create a global distributed network

WAN DIVERSITY

The world’s most flexible and diverse WAN options from cloud configurable GE WAN ports to integrated 4G and 5G and even Wi-Fi as a WAN

Global L2 and L3 Networks

Easily create global L2, L3 or a mix of L2 and L3 Networks which can span Multiple Clouds and Hubs. Multiple VRF support and policy based routing ensures isolation and security.

Application Optimization: MapleCloud SD-WAN optimizes application performance by prioritizing critical traffic and applying quality of service (QoS) policies. This ensures that bandwidth is allocated efficiently, enhancing user experience and productivity. Scalability and Flexibility: Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, MapleCloud SD-WAN scales to meet your evolving needs. With support for a wide range of deployment scenarios, including branch offices, remote sites, and cloud environments, the solution adapts seamlessly to your organization's growth and requirements. Visibility and Analytics: Gain deep insights into your network performance with MapleCloud SD-WAN's comprehensive analytics and reporting capabilities. Track key metrics, identify trends, and troubleshoot issues in real-time, empowering informed decision-making and optimization.
